ECSE Virtual Capstone Design Expo

The objective of the ECSE Capstone Expo is to showcase and celebrate the R & D achievements of our graduating students from the Electrical Engineering and Software Engineering programs. It is an opportunity for our community to gather together to discover the engineering artifacts produced by students.

The ECSE Capstone Expo was scheduled for Friday, April 3 @ 10:00am - 2:00pm, but due to COVID-19 (novel Coronavirus) the in-person event is cancelled. But our students have been working very hard on their Capstone projects over the past two semesters (Sept 2019 - March 2020), and we want to showcase their work to fellow students, faculty & staff, family & friends, and the rest of the community & the world.
